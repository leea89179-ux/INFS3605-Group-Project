## I would also add this (very important for your app)

Since your app is **medical**, add the following section:

> **Medical Content Requirements**
>
> * Do not simplify or alter the clinical meaning of Familial Hypercholesterolaemia (FH).
> * Maintain medically accurate terminology across all languages.
> * When no established local medical translation exists, preserve the English medical term alongside the translated explanation.
> * Ensure all educational content remains understandable for patients with varying health literacy while keeping medical accuracy.
> * Do not translate medication names, laboratory values, or universally recognized clinical abbreviations unless an official localized equivalent exists.

This helps prevent the AI from introducing inaccurate or misleading translations, which is especially important in a healthcare application.
